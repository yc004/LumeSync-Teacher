# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and inclusive.
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Personal attacks or trolling.
- Publishing others'' private information.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Reporting

Please report violations to the maintainers via repository issues or security contact channels.
